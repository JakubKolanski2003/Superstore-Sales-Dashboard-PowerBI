# Superstore Sales Dashboard 📊💼

An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing sales, profit, and customer performance for a fictional US retail chain (Superstore) from 2014 to 2017.

## 📊 Dashboard Overview

This project consists of 4 pages:

### 1. Sales Overview
- Monthly Sales Trend with Year-over-Year comparison
- Top 10 Products by Sales
- Key KPIs: Total Sales, Total Profit, Total Orders, Profit Margin

### 2. Regional Analysis
- Sales by Region (West, East, Central, South)
- Sales by Category (Technology, Furniture, Office Supplies)
- Top 10 States by Sales

### 3. Customer Performance
- Top 10 Customers by Sales
- Top 10 Customers by Profit
- Sales vs Profit Scatter Plot
- Sales by Segment (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office)
- Year Slicer for interactive filtering

### 4. Category Details (Drill-through)
- Detailed Sub-Category breakdown
- Accessible via drill-through from other pages

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- **Power BI Desktop** — data modeling and visualization
- **Power Query (M Code)** — data cleaning, mixed date formats handling
- **DAX** — custom measures and calculations

## 💡 Skills Demonstrated
- Star Schema — Date Table with relationships
- DAX: CALCULATE, DIVIDE, DISTINCTCOUNT, RANKX
- Time Intelligence: SAMEPERIODLASTYEAR (YoY %)
- Drill-through navigation
- Bookmarks
- Dynamic titles
- Top N filtering
- Conditional formatting

## 📈 Key Insights
- Total Sales: $2.20M across 4 years
- Profit Margin: 12%
- West region leads in sales (~$725K)
- Technology is the top category (37% of sales)
- Consumer segment accounts for 50% of revenue
